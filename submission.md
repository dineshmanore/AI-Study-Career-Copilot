## 📝 Project Overview
**Scholarly Edge** is an all-in-one AI-powered workspace designed specifically for students and early-career professionals. It functions as an "AI Study & Career Copilot." The system completely integrates MERN-stack architecture with advanced AI endpoints to provide an intellectual atelier holding diverse academic tools. 

Key technical aspects include:
- A responsive, glassmorphic UI built with Tailwind CSS.
- Semantic Dark/Light Modes engineered entirely through CSS mapping.
- Authentication wall defending guest modules versus Pro features.
- MongoDB data persistence across user contexts.

## 📸 Detailed Application Modules

### 1. The Global Dashboard
Offers a responsive statistics bento-grid, tracking tasks completed, flashcards reviewed, study streaks, and overall academic progression.
![Global Dashboard](./screenshots/dashboard_desktop_1775226000626.png)

### 2. Strategic Task Management
A beautifully refined modal view allowing students to queue initiatives, define urgency matrices, and set targeted completion epochs. 
![Task Manager](./screenshots/tasks_modal_desktop_1775226032789.png)

### 3. AI Study Planner
An intelligence module that maps out structured daily/weekly tracks based on exam dates, hours targeted, and subject difficulty.
![Study Plan Builder](./screenshots/study_plan_desktop_1775226749796.png)

### 4. Intelligent Notes Center
Convert heavy lecture notes or raw materials into categorized bullet points using instant AI-powered "Quick Summary" generation.
![Notes Hub](./screenshots/notes_desktop_1775226768536.png)

### 5. Chat & Ask AI module
A live, fast streaming tutor offering to resolve lingering study doubts, explain code, or quiz the student contextually based on their history.
![Doubt Solver](./screenshots/ask_ai_desktop_1775227209298.png)

### 6. ATS Resume Architect
Designed to bridge the gap into the professional world. The Resume builder actively grades your impact bullets through an ATS lens and outputs actionable refinement tips.
![Resume Analyzer](./screenshots/resume_desktop_1775226771821.png)

### 7. Interview Preparator
On-demand mock interview simulation spanning behavioral grids or specific technical topics, offering graded evaluations of your text answers.
![Interview Room](./screenshots/interview_desktop_1775227222163.png)

### 8. User Identity Hub (Profile)
A dedicated management area tracking user security and "Scholarly Impact" parameters—a visualization of their engagement with the software.
![Profile Settings](./screenshots/profile_desktop_1775226045300.png)

### 9. System Configurations (Settings)
Controls global preferences like swapping AI LLM backends on the fly (Gemini/Qwen), adjusting communication priorities, and toggling visual modes.
![Configuration Panel](./screenshots/settings_ai_desktop_1775226066767.png)
